Cédric Girerd

CNRS Researcher

LIRMM, Montpellier, France

Cédric Girerd is a CNRS Researcher at LIRMM in Montpellier, France. He obtained his PhD from the University of Strasbourg in 2018, and Engineering Degree (≈ M.Sc.) from SIGMA Clermont and a Research Master (≈ M.Sc.) both in 2014 from the Blaise-Pascal University in Clermont-Ferrand.

His research interests include the design, instrumentation and control of continuum robots and soft robots for medical applications, such as minimally invasive surgery. He is part of the DEXTER Team at LIRMM, which conducts research in medical and industrial robots.
