Cédric Girerd

CNRS Researcher

LIRMM, Montpellier, France

👋 Welcome! I am a CNRS Researcher in Robotics.
I work in the DEXTER Team at LIRMM in Montpellier, France.
My group focuses on the design and control of continuum, soft and inflatable robots for medical and industrial applications.
